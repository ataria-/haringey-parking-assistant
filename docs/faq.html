Frequently Asked Questions

Haringey Multi-Day Parking Extension — book visitor permits faster, with less hassle.

Important: This is an independent tool and is not made by or affiliated with Haringey Council (or the permit website provider). It simply helps automate the same steps you would do manually on the official portal.

Why I built this

I live in Haringey and during a building project I had to book visitor permits constantly for over 7 months, for different cars. The portal only allows booking one day at a time — which meant repeating the same steps over and over. I lost quite a lot of time, made mistakes and got a couple of fines. So I decided to fix the problem myself and turn it into a simple tool others can benefit from too.

What does this extension do?

It helps you book multiple days of Haringey visitor parking permits in one flow instead of repeating the same booking process day-by-day. You select a date range, pick your vehicle, and the extension guides/automates the bookings.

Who is this useful for?

Anyone who regularly books visitor permits, especially:

  • Building works / trades: book a week (or more) without daily repetition.
  • Family visits: grandparents or relatives staying for several days.
  • Friends staying over: long weekends, holidays, or frequent visitors.
  • Busy households: reduce last-minute stress and avoid accidental gaps.

What’s the main use case?

Multi-day booking. The portal makes you repeat the same actions for each day. This extension reduces that friction, so it’s easier to stay compliant and avoid mistakes.

Does it help avoid unnecessary bookings?

Yes. The extension can help you avoid booking permits when they aren’t needed (for example, outside restricted hours or on days that don’t require a permit). This saves time and can help avoid wasting vouchers.

Is it safe to use?

The extension only runs on the official Haringey permit website. It does not store your login details. Your saved vehicles and preferences are stored locally in your browser. Subscription access is managed by ExtensionPay (payment processing via Stripe).

Will it always work?

The extension depends on the permit website’s layout and behavior. If the council/provider updates the site, the extension may stop working temporarily. If that happens, please report it — I actively maintain the extension and aim to fix issues quickly.

How much does it cost?

You get 3 free booking sessions to make sure it works for you and to see the benefits. After that, it’s £3/month.

The fee helps cover development time and running costs, and is typically less than the cost of just a few hours of paid parking/permits — especially compared to the cost of a single fine.

What’s included in the free trial vs Pro?

Free trial: Multi-day booking (up to 3 booking sessions)
Pro: Unlimited multi-day bookings, save multiple vehicles, save favourite times, and smarter skipping of days/times that don’t require a permit

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through the subscription/payment flow (Stripe via ExtensionPay). Your Pro access continues until the end of your billing period.

Something isn’t working — what do I do?

First, refresh the Haringey permit page and try again. If the problem persists, please get in touch and include: (1) what you were trying to do, (2) what happened, and (3) a screenshot if possible.
