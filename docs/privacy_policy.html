Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This privacy policy explains how the Haringey Multi-Day Parking Chrome extension handles your data.

Not affiliated: This extension is an independent tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Haringey Council or the permit website provider.
Summary: Your saved vehicles and preferences are stored locally in your browser. The extension communicates with:
  • haringey.tarantopermits.com to help submit permit bookings you request
  • extensionpay.com to manage the free trial and subscription status
We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

What data does this extension store?

The extension stores the following data locally in Chrome extension storage:

  • Saved vehicles: Vehicle labels/names and registration numbers you choose to save
  • Preferences: Your preferred booking start and end times (and similar settings)
  • Trial/subscription status: Information needed to remember whether premium is enabled (e.g., an entitlement flag or identifier)

This locally stored data is used only to provide the extension’s features. It is not uploaded to any server operated by the developer.

What data does this extension access on the permit website?

When you are on the Haringey permit booking pages, the extension reads information needed to assist with booking, such as:

  • Your selected parking zone and permit options shown on the page
  • Your remaining voucher balance shown on the page
  • Booking form fields required to submit bookings

This access is used to automate the booking workflow you initiate and to prevent unnecessary bookings.

What data does this extension send?

1) Data sent to haringey.tarantopermits.com

The extension sends booking form data to haringey.tarantopermits.com to complete bookings you request. This is the same type of data you would enter manually on the site (e.g., vehicle registration, dates/times).

2) Data sent to extensionpay.com (subscriptions)

If you use the free trial or purchase a subscription, the extension communicates with extensionpay.com to verify trial/subscription status and to enable subscription management.

The extension does not send your parking booking details (zones, dates, voucher balance, etc.) to ExtensionPay as part of subscription checks.

Payments and subscription processing

Subscriptions are processed by ExtensionPay, which uses Stripe for payment processing.

  • When you purchase a subscription, ExtensionPay collects your email address and stores payment information securely in Stripe.
  • The developer does not receive your full payment card details.

See their policies:

  • ExtensionPay
  • Stripe

Permissions explained

  • storage: Saves your vehicles, preferences, and trial/subscription status locally
  • activeTab: Allows the extension to interact with the permit site tab while you are using it
  • host permissions (haringey.tarantopermits.com): Allows the extension to run on the permit booking pages
  • host permissions (extensionpay.com): Allows the extension to verify trial/subscription status and open/manage checkout flows

Data retention

Your locally stored vehicles and preferences remain on your device until you delete them in the extension or uninstall the extension.

Subscription purchase history may be retained by ExtensionPay/Stripe as part of payment processing and records.

Data deletion

To delete data stored by this extension on your device:

  • Remove saved vehicles using the extension UI (if provided), and/or
  • Clear the extension’s data via Chrome extension settings, or uninstall the extension (which removes local extension data)

If you have a paid subscription, you can manage or cancel it through the ExtensionPay subscription flow.

Limited use

Any data accessed by the extension is used only to provide the extension’s single purpose (helping you book and manage Haringey visitor parking permits) and as described in this policy.

Changes to this policy
